My Background

My professional experience started in science. I studied biochemistry in college which then led me to work in a clinial lab for a few years. As much as I love science I desired a field with more opportunity to grow on a day to day basis. Something that integrated my insatiable passion for art and the utlization of my analytical mind. I worked a lot with technology in he lab which led me to start taking on computer science on the side. I realized how endless the field was so I started working on little projects on my own. I took a complete professional pivot and decided to take on a software development bootcamp full time. There I attended 450+ hours of hand on coding experience with Java, Javascript, CSS, and SQL. I learned about agile methodologies and how to successfully deploy various projects utilizing SDLC. The hours were long and workload unforgiving if not tackled 100%. That leap of faith off as I was able to achieve valedictorian of my class. That validation reassured me that I was on the right path. Since then I have launched my personal portfolio using json and wordpress. My proudest feat was producing an application for yoga instructors implementing CRUD methodologies for yoga instructors to create yoga sequences. I hope to continuously expand my experience in the software development world, help others, and never stop learning along the way.
